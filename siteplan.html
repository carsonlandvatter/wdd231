Site Name: Loopify

Name was given because people can loop through songs and preview them before adding them to a playlist.

Site Purpose

For users to be able to preview music and add it to their library on a separate page.

Scenarios

  • What are some popular songs to listen to in the US?
  • How can I make a good playlist for my workouts and studies?

Color Schema

  • logo color/accents: #ffc800
  • accent2 color: rgb(255, 94, 121)
  • text color: #b1b1b1;

Typography
